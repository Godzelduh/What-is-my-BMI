What is my current weight and height?

    BMI stands for body mass index, and this has been a useful tool since as far as back as the 1940s!

    Your BMI is determined by using your height and weight, and the results will tell if you are under, over or at the ideal weight!

    The higher your BMI is, the more at risk you could be for underlying weight related issues, so keep close attention!
